On the Insert tab, the galleries include items that are designed to coordinate with the overall look of your document. You can use these galleries to insert tables, headers, footers, lists, cover pages, and other document building blocks. When you create pictures, charts, or diagrams, they also coordinate with your current document look. You can easily change the formatting of selected text in the document by choosing a look from the Quick Styles gallery on the Home tab. You can also make text stand out by using the other controls on the Home tab. Most controls provide a live preview, so you can always see what you document will look like before you apply the change.
